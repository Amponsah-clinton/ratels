How It All Began

Our Origin Story

Founded in the wake of systematic oppression and silenced voices, RATEL emerged as a collective response to the growing need for documented truth and organized resistance.

What started as a small group of concerned citizens has grown into a movement of thousands, united by a common purpose: to hold power accountable and amplify the voices of the marginalized.

The Spirit of the Honey Badger

Why "Ratel"?

The ratel, commonly known as the honey badger, is renowned for its fearlessness. Despite its small size, it takes on opponents many times its weight, never backing down from a fight.

This spirit embodies our movement: relentless, fearless, and unstoppable in our pursuit of truth and justice.

How We Create Change

Our Approach

We believe in the power of documentation, education, and collective action. Our approach combines:

  • Evidence-Based Advocacy — Every claim backed by verifiable facts
  • Grassroots Mobilization — Change starts from the ground up
  • Digital Resistance — Leveraging technology for transparency
  • Community Building — Strength in unity and solidarity
